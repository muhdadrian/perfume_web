About Us

Al-Qamar Oud, a small perfume shop has been in perfume business selling Arabian fragrance with multiple scents since April 2021 in Sabah before relocating to Labuan Island (Federal Territory) on May at the same year.


It does serve the customers around the island as well as in Sabah. We do have our kiosk located in Masjid Biru Lajau, Labuan where the customers can walk-in and purchase our products.
